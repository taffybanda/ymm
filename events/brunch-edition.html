The second Young Moguls Mixer (Brunch Edition) which was held at The Venue on the 15th of February 2025, was more than just a networking event. It was a call to action for young visionaries to ignite their empires, craft their legacy, and build with intent even when no one is watching.

Tutsirai Jenje led the charge, challenging us to rethink what visibility truly means through her talk on "Building Your Signature Legacy Brand even when no one knows your name." She reminded us that branding is not about clout. It is about consistency, authenticity, and purpose. The spotlight does not make your brand. The foundation you build in the shadows does.

Her words hit home,

“Do not wait to be discovered. Build like you are already known.”

Own your story.

Position your brand with intention.

Show up even when no one is clapping.

It was a wake-up call for every Young Mogul in the room to build with a long-term vision in mind, to craft a legacy brand that speaks for itself, regardless of the size of the audience today.

Tinotenda Borerwe took the conversation deeper with "Social Capital: Building Real Connections That Drive Growth."

He reminded us that your network is not just your net worth. It is your mirror, your momentum, and your map. True success does not come from just “knowing the right people,” but from nurturing relationships that are authentic, consistent, and mutually empowering.

This year’s mixer was not just inspiring. It was deeply personal. Since it was right after Valentine’s Day, the founder Nicole Duma added a heartfelt twist.

For the singles in the room (which was pretty a large portion of the room), we were serenaded by a saxophonist who played for every single person. He did not just stand in one corner. He walked through the room, interacting with everyone, making it feel like each person had their own private “mukomana webhosvo” moment.

And just when we thought it could not get any sweeter, we had Young Moguls Mixer Postcards. Each person wrote an encouraging note which was then shuffled and given to someone else. That moment of receiving encouragement from a stranger-turned-community-member? Absolutely priceless!!!!!

The Young Moguls Mixer is not your typical sit-in-a-row, stare-at-a-PowerPoint event. It is a space where young people are seen, heard, and pushed to do better. It is about shaping each other, exchanging energy, and leaving with lessons that are deeply personal yet universally empowering.

This edition reminded us to:

  • Build your brand like you are already known.
  • Build your network like you will grow together for life.
  • Ignite your empire because no one will build it for you.

By Ruth Tsanga
