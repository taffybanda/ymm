The Launch Edition

Launching a Movement

The event that marked the beginning of what would become a growing community of ambitious young people who are committed to personal and professional growth.

The word launch is defined by the Oxford English Dictionary as "to start or set in motion an activity, project, or enterprise." A launch signifies the beginning of something new, exciting, and full of potential. It marks the first step of a journey and creates an opportunity for growth, innovation, and impact.

For many people, youth is often associated with fun, entertainment, and the pursuit of a comfortable lifestyle. While there is nothing wrong with enjoying life, there is another side to today's young people that is often overlooked. Across Zimbabwe, young people are actively searching for spaces where they can connect with like-minded individuals, build meaningful networks, form partnerships, learn from experienced professionals, and access opportunities that can help shape their futures.

Some are entrepreneurs with innovative business ideas. Others are emerging leaders who are eager to make a difference in their communities. Many are professionals looking to grow in their careers, while others are simply curious learners seeking knowledge and inspiration. Despite this diversity, one common challenge remained and that is the lack of intentional spaces designed to bring ambitious young people together.

Recognising this gap, Nicole Duma, Founder of Young Moguls, envisioned a platform that would create meaningful connections among young people while providing practical knowledge, inspiration, and access to opportunities. From this vision, the Young Moguls Mixer was born. At the heart of the Young Moguls vision is the powerful message found in 1 Timothy 4:12, "Let no one despise or think less of you because of your youth, but be an example (pattern) for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity."

On 21 June 2024, that vision became a reality with the successful hosting of the Young Moguls Mixer Launch Edition. The event marked the beginning of what would become a growing community of ambitious young people who are committed to personal and professional growth.

The launch edition brought together young entrepreneurs, professionals, students, innovators, and aspiring leaders from different sectors. The atmosphere was filled with energy, curiosity, and a shared desire to learn, connect, and grow.

A key highlight of the event was the insightful presentations delivered by distinguished guest speakers who challenged attendees to think differently about opportunities and success.

Opportunities for Professional and Personal Growth for Young People in Zimbabwe

Guest speaker Joshua Masundah delivered an inspiring presentation on "Opportunities for Professional and Personal Growth for Young People in Zimbabwe." He encouraged young people to take ownership of their development by actively seeking opportunities for learning, mentorship, networking, and skills development.

His presentation highlighted the importance of continuous self-improvement, resilience, and intentional career planning. Attendees were reminded that growth does not happen by chance but it happens through deliberate effort, strategic relationships, and a willingness to embrace new opportunities.

Unlocking Opportunities: Alternative Financing and Growing Your Business in the Digital Era

The second keynote presentation was delivered by John Sakala, who spoke on "Unlocking Opportunities: Alternative Financing and Growing Your Business in the Digital Era."

His session provided valuable insights into the evolving business landscape and explored innovative financing options available to entrepreneurs beyond traditional banking systems. He also highlighted the transformative power of digital platforms and technology in helping businesses access new markets, attract customers, and scale their operations.

Participants gained practical knowledge on leveraging digital tools, embracing innovation, and positioning their businesses for sustainable growth in an increasingly connected world.

More Than an Event

The Young Moguls Mixer Launch Edition was more than just an event. It was the beginning of a movement. It demonstrated the power of bringing together ambitious young people who are committed to learning from one another, sharing experiences, and creating opportunities collectively.

The connections made, ideas shared, and lessons learnt on that day laid the foundation for a growing network of young leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals who are shaping the future of Zimbabwe.

As Young Moguls continues to grow, the vision remains clear and that is to create a thriving community where young people can learn, connect, collaborate, and unlock their full potential.

By Ruth Tsanga
