Our Founder

Nicole K. Duma

Founder & Visionary

An award-winning Business Development and Growth professional, thought leader, speaker, and community builder passionate about empowering young professionals, intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs to reach their full potential. She has built a career centered on strategic relationship building, leadership, and sustainable growth.

As the Founder of Young Moguls Mixer, Nicole leads a growing community dedicated to fostering meaningful connections, professional development, and collaboration among emerging leaders. Through her work, she champions the idea that success is accelerated through intentional networking, continuous learning, and the courage to pursue purpose-driven opportunities.
